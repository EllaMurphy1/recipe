Five Willow Fish Recipe

Serves 4

ingredients:

  • 1 whole mullet or sea bass, bream, trout, or carp, weighing about 1 1/2 Ilbs after cleaning
  • 1 tsp salt
  • About 2 tbs all-purpose flour
  • 5 cups vegetable oil for deep-frying
  • 2-3 dried mushrooms, soaked and thinly shredded
  • 1 small carrot, thinly shredded
  • 1 small green bellpepper, cored, seeded, and thinly shredded
  • 3 scallions, thinly shredded
  • 1 tb thinly shredded fresh ginger root
  • 2 tbs sugar
  • 3 tbs rice vinegar
  • 1 tb light soy sauce
  • 1 tb rice wine
  • About 1/2 cup Chinese Stock (page 12) or water
  • 2 tsps cornstarch mixed with 1 tb water
  • 1/1 tsp sesasame oil
